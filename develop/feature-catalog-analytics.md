# Feature Catalog: Analytics & Insights

Data-driven feedback to help users understand their performance and refine their strategy.

## 1. Unified Dashboard
*   **Aggregated Metrics:** View total reach, engagement, and follower growth across all connected accounts in a single workspace.
*   **Comparative Analysis:** Compare performance between different time periods (e.g., this week vs. last week).

## 2. Platform-Specific Reports
*   **Granular Metrics:** Drill down into specific platform data (e.g., Twitter Retweets, Instagram Likes, Mastodon Boosts).
*   **Best Time to Post:** Insights derived from historical engagement data suggesting optimal posting windows.

## 3. Individual Post Performance
*   **Engagement Tracking:** Track the success of individual posts to identify what types of content resonate best with the audience.
*   **External Links Tracking:** Integration with URL shorteners or UTM parameters to track click-through rates (CTR) from social media to external websites.
