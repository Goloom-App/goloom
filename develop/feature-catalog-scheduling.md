# Feature Catalog: Scheduling & Automation

This module manages the timing and delivery of content, ensuring a consistent presence on social media.

## 1. Visual Content Calendar
*   **Drag-and-Drop Interface:** A monthly and weekly view of all scheduled posts, allowing users to move posts between dates visually.
*   **Status Indicators:** Visual cues for post status (Draft, Scheduled, Published, Failed).
*   **Filtering:** Ability to filter the calendar by workspace, team member, or social platform.

## 2. Queue & Posting Schedules
*   **Time Slots:** Define specific recurring time slots for each day of the week where content should be published.
*   **Auto-Queueing:** Automatically assign the next available time slot to a new post, maintaining a steady stream of content without manual scheduling.
*   **Bulk Scheduling:** Upload multiple posts via CSV or specialized interface to fill the schedule quickly.

## 3. Smart Automations
*   **Error Recovery:** Automatic notification and manual "retry" capability for posts that fail due to API issues or platform temporary downtime.
