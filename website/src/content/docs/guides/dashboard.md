The **dashboard** is the home screen for a team. It gives you a quick overview of
what's scheduled, what recently published, and how your accounts are doing.

## What you'll see

- **Upcoming posts** — the next scheduled items across all connected accounts.
- **Recent activity** — posts that have published or changed state.
- **Account health** — connected providers and any that need re-authentication.
- **Quick links** into the composer, calendar and analytics.

## Switching teams

If you belong to more than one team, use the team switcher to change context.
All content, accounts and analytics are **scoped to the selected team**.
