The **media library** stores images and assets per team so they're one click
away inside the [composer](/guides/composer/).

## Why it exists

- Upload an asset once and reuse it across many posts.
- Keep brand imagery consistent across accounts.
- Avoid re-uploading the same files for every post.

## Using it

1. Open the team's **Media library**.
2. Upload files, or browse what's already there.
3. From the composer, attach media by picking from the library.

Uploaded media is stored alongside your database (on the volume you mounted in
[installation](/getting-started/installation/) when running with Docker).
