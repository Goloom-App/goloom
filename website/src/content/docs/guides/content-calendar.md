The **content calendar** is the planning surface for everything your team has
scheduled. It shows posts across every connected account in one place.

## Views

- A **grid** calendar view for the month/week at a glance.
- Each entry shows its target accounts and scheduled time.

## Rescheduling

Move a post to a new slot to **reschedule** it. Times are **timezone-aware**, so
what you plan is what publishes.

## Cancelling

Cancel a scheduled post from the calendar before it publishes. Over the API,
posts also expose a cancel action under
`/v1/teams/{teamID}/posts`.

## Working ahead

Combine the calendar with [automation](/guides/automation/) (recurring templates
and RSS rules) to keep channels active without planning every post by hand.
