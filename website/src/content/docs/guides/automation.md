Automation lets goloom keep your accounts active without manual work for every
single post.

## Recurring post templates

Define a template that publishes on a schedule — for example weekly or monthly
patterns. goloom creates the scheduled posts for you, which you can still review
and adjust on the [calendar](/guides/content-calendar/).

## RSS feed rules

Point a rule at an RSS feed and goloom can **auto-import** new items as draft or
scheduled posts. This is ideal for syndicating a blog or news source to the
fediverse.

## Combining with review

When your team uses approvals, automated posts can flow into the
[review queue](/guides/review-queue/) so a human signs off before anything is
published.
