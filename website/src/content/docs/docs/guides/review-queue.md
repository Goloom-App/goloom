---
title: Review queue
description: Approve, schedule, or discard automation drafts before they publish.
---

The **Review queue** holds drafts that automation created but that should not publish without human approval — for example posts from [RSS feeds](/docs/guides/automation/) or AI-enhanced recurring templates.

The sidebar badge shows how many items are waiting. Overdue items are highlighted when their suggested time has passed.

## What appears here

Typical sources:

- **RSS imports** — new articles converted to draft posts
- **Automation with review** — recurring or feed rules configured to require approval
- **AI-generated content** — when auto-publish is disabled on the voice profile

Each row shows a preview, target accounts, suggested schedule time, and an **Automation** badge when applicable.

## Actions

For each draft you can:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Edit** | Open the post in the [Composer](/docs/guides/composer/) |
| **Publish now** | Send immediately to connected providers |
| **Schedule** | Pick a date and time, then confirm |
| **Discard** | Delete the draft (confirmation required) |

**Editors** and **owners** can perform all actions. **Viewers** can read the queue but cannot approve or publish.

## Workflow tips

1. Process overdue items first — reschedule or publish rather than leaving them stale.
2. Edit in the composer when AI or RSS templates need a human touch.
3. Empty queue? Check [Automation](/docs/guides/automation/) rules still point at active feeds or templates.
