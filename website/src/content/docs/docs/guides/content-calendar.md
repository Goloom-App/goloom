title: Content calendar

The **Content calendar** is the visual planning view for your workspace. Posts appear as tiles on a monthly or weekly grid so you can see density, gaps, and timing at a glance.

## Calendar grid

Each tile represents a scheduled or draft post. You can:

- **Drag and drop** posts to a new date or time (when you have editor or owner access).
- Open a post to edit it in the [Composer](/docs/guides/composer/).

Media-rich posts show thumbnails; text-only posts show a short snippet.

## Filters

Use the sidebar filters to narrow what appears on the grid:

- **Platform** — show only Mastodon, Bluesky, Friendica, and so on.
- **Account** — focus on one connected account.

Filters are useful when one workspace manages many destinations.

## Time slots and queue

Highlighted regions on the calendar can mark **optimal posting times** or **queue slots** configured for your team. Recurring templates and RSS automation may also pre-fill slots ahead of time — see [Automation](/docs/guides/automation/).

## Workflow tips

1. Scan the week for empty days before creating new content.
2. Move conflicting posts instead of deleting and recreating them.
3. Open overdue items from the [Review queue](/docs/guides/review-queue/) and schedule them directly on the calendar.
