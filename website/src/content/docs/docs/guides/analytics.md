title: Analytics
description: Track reach, engagement, follower growth, and top-performing posts.
---

The **Analytics** view shows how your content and accounts perform over time. Metrics are synced from connected providers on a schedule; administrators can trigger a manual sync from the [Administration](/docs/admin/administration/) panel.

## Metric selectors

Toggle between views such as:

- **Total reach** — how many people saw your content
- **Engagement rate** — interactions relative to impressions
- **Follower growth** — audience size over time

Exact labels may vary by provider; Mastodon, Friendica, and Bluesky each expose compatible engagement fields where available.

## Charts

Time-series line charts and bar graphs compare platforms and accounts. Use them to answer:

- Which network drives the most engagement this month?
- Did a campaign spike on one account but not another?

## Top performing posts

A sortable list ranks posts by engagement (likes, reposts, replies, and similar signals). Open a row to jump back to the post in the composer or calendar.

## External posts

If **Monitor external posts** is enabled in [Team settings](/docs/guides/teams/), goloom imports posts published outside the app and tracks their engagement here. Useful for a complete picture when you sometimes post directly on a provider.

## Permissions

All team members with at least **viewer** access can read analytics for their workspace.
