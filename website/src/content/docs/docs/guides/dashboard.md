The **Dashboard** is your home screen after you sign in. It gives a quick read on what is scheduled next, how recent posts performed, and whether connected accounts need attention.

## Upcoming posts

The dashboard lists your next scheduled posts (typically the next 5–10), with:

- Scheduled date and time
- Target platform icons
- Post title or a text preview

Use this section to spot gaps in your calendar or confirm that automation created the drafts you expect.

## Recent performance

Mini charts summarize engagement trends over the last seven days. They help you see whether reach and interaction are moving up or down without opening the full analytics view.

## Account status

Connected accounts appear in a grid with status indicators:

- **Active** — credentials are valid and the account is ready to publish.
- **Needs re-auth** — the provider token expired or was revoked; reconnect the account under [Accounts](/docs/guides/accounts/).

## Tips

- Select a **workspace** in the header before reading the dashboard — all numbers and lists are scoped to that team.
- If metrics look stale, an administrator can trigger a sync from the [Administration](/docs/admin/administration/) panel.
